Bioinformatics and Genetics Researcher

van Haren Laboratory

My research aims to discover the immunometabolic effects of vitamin D in children with X-ALD, a significant genetic mimic of multiple sclerosis. Using a two-pronged approach through digital gene set enrichment analysis and tissue culture, I hope to shed light on the pathways and expression patterns that give rise to neurodegenerative disease.
